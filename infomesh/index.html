InfoMesh

Introducing "InfoMesh" - A Collaborative CDR and IPDR Analysis Software by Shweta Labs and IIT Kharagpur.

Discover "InfoMesh," an innovative and collaborative CDR and IPDR Analysis Software jointly crafted by Shweta Labs Pvt. Ltd. and IIT Kharagpur With cutting-edge features and seamless integration of telecommunication data, "InfoMesh" provides unparalleled insights and solutions for your communication analysis needs.

01.

Unmatched Insights

"InfoMesh" offers an intuitive GUI and comprehensive analysis capabilities, allowing you to explore call patterns, session behaviours, tower data, common contacts, IMEI analysis, SMS patterns, geo-location, movements, IP geolocation, and more. Its customizable filters and queries provide tailored solutions, enhancing operational efficiency and streamlining investigations.

02.

Empowering Law Enforcement

Developed in partnership with IIT Kharagpur, "InfoMesh" empowers law enforcement agencies and armed forces to combat crime and bolster national security. Its visualisation and reporting features simplify data presentation, facilitating seamless collaboration among investigative teams.

03.

Value of Collaboration

The collaboration between IIT Kharagpur and Shweta Labs elevates the credibility of "InfoMesh." Leveraging IIT Kharagpur's academic prowess and Shweta Labs' industry expertise, this software stands as a powerful and reliable tool for insightful communication data analysis.

04.

Stay Ahead with InfoMesh

As a trusted name in the industry, Shweta Labs ensures data privacy and security, guaranteeing confidential handling of sensitive information. With continuous support and updates, "InfoMesh" stays current with evolving needs, making it the ultimate choice for effective communication data analysis.

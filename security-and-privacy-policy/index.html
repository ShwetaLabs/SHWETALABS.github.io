Security & Privacy Policy

01.

Privacy Policy

Introduction:

Welcome to Shweta Labs! At Shweta Labs, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and services.

Information Collected: We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, and contact details, when you interact with our website or submit forms for inquiries or subscriptions. Additionally, we may gather non-personal information through cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience.

Use of Information:

Your information may be used to process your inquiries, fulfil service requests, and provide you with relevant updates and communications. We do not share your personal data with third parties without your consent unless required by law.

Data Security:

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure. Your information is stored securely on our servers.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies:

We use cookies to enhance your user experience on our website. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Third-Party Links:

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. Please note that we are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites.

User Choices and Access:

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you have any questions or requests regarding your data, please contact us using the details provided below.

Updates to Privacy Policy:

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. The latest version will be effective upon posting on this page, along with the revision date.

02.

Security Policy:

Data Protection Commitment:

At Shweta Labs, we are dedicated to protecting your data and ensuring its confidentiality and integrity.

Data Encryption:

We utilise industry-standard encryption technologies to secure data during transmission, safeguarding it from unauthorised interception.

Access Controls:

Access to your personal information is limited to authorised personnel who require it to perform specific tasks.

Regular Audits:

We conduct regular security audits to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our systems.

User Responsibilities:

We advise users to maintain the security of their accounts by using strong passwords and refraining from sharing login credentials with others.
